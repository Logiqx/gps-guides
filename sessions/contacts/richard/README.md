## Richard's Tracks

### Garmin Vivoactive 3

A couple of sessions from Richard and Max who both have the Vivoactive3.

This watch uses the same Sony GPS as the Fenix 6, COROS Apex Pro, etc.



#### Speed Resolution

Confirmed speed resolution to be 1 cm/s.



#### Doppler vs Positional

Used Max's session (top 20 runs over 250m) to see that non-Doppler is always fastest:

- Average of 0.36 knots faster
- Maximum of 0.76 knots faster
