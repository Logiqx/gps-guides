## 11 April 2022

### Summary

#### Overview

WIndsurfing session in south easterly winds. Portland Harbour, UK.

This session was record for subsequent analysis of acceleration and mAcc filters on 5Hz / 10Hz devices.



#### Devices

- COROS APEX Pro (1 Hz) - firmware V2.66.0 - left wrist under wetsuit.
- Locosys GW-60 (5 Hz) - firmware V1.3A0926B - right wrist over wetsuit.
- Locosys GW-52 (5 Hz) and GT-31 (1 Hz) - stored in Aquapac on right bicep.
  - GW-52 - firmware V1.2 G0529C - bottom of Aquapac, oriented upwards.
  - GW-31 - firmware V1.4 B0803T - top of Aquapac, oriented upwards with screen flipped.
- Motion Mini (10 Hz) - firmware 3068 - left bicep.
